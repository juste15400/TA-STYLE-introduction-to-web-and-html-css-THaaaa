Business Marketing is practicing of individual,or organisations,including commercial businesses,governments and institutions,facilitating the sale of their products or services to order companies or organizations that in turn resell them,use them as component in products or services they offer,or use them to support their operations.Also known as industrial marketin,business marketing is also called business to business marketing or b2b marketing for short. (Note that while marketing to government entities shares some of the same dynamics of organizational marketing,B2B Marketing is meaning different.
